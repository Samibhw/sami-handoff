Our story

Built on the idea that selling a house should be simple and fair

Cash House Buyers 4 You was founded on a simple belief: when life throws you a situation where you need to sell — inherited property, divorce, relocation, foreclosure, tired rental, or just tired of dealing with a house — you deserve a fair, transparent cash offer and a path to closing that doesn't take 90 days and eat 8% of your equity.

We've been in business since 2019. We've purchased hundreds of homes. We're BBB A+ Accredited. We close with Closeline Settlements, a national title company — so your proceeds are held in licensed escrow, not on a handshake. And we do it with real people answering real phone calls — not automated lowball algorithms and not high-pressure scripts.

What sets us apart from a traditional cash buyer: our buyer network. We maintain a database of hedge funds, private family offices, and professional investors actively looking for properties in our markets. When we take a listing under contract, we have multiple verified buyers competing — which often gets sellers significantly more than a single cash offer would. And on select higher-value properties, we also offer novation deals — listing on the MLS on your behalf and splitting the retail upside with you. Traditional cash buyers can't offer that. Neither can traditional agents.

Our approach to today's market: The real estate market has slowed dramatically since 2022. Homes sit for 6–12+ months. Buyers are picky. Sellers are accepting prices they hate or watching listings rot. In this environment, the structure of a sale matters more than the buyer — and we're one of the few companies in our markets offering five different structures (cash, seller financing, subject-to, wholesale through our investor network, or novation). When a seller calls us, we don't just pitch cash. We walk through all five options, compare what each nets them in their specific situation, and help them pick the structure that actually fits their life — not just what's fastest for us to close.

Our team is based in Atlanta, with acquisitions and dispositions covering Georgia, Oklahoma, Florida, and Washington. When you reach out, you're talking to someone who knows your market, knows your situation, and will give you the straight story on what your house is worth to us — and whether selling to us is actually the right move for you.
