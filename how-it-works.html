What makes us different

Selling your home can be a quick and easy process

Cash House Buyers 4 You buys houses in Atlanta, Tulsa, Tampa Bay (Hernando & Pasco counties), and Seattle. We're not listing your house — we're actually the ones buying your home.

Because we pay cash and we're buying directly from you, we're able to close quickly — or on your schedule.

When you work with us, there are no fees and no commissions like there are when you list with an agent. You don't have to worry about extra costs, paying out of pocket to sell, or getting your house ready for a sale. We'll buy your house as-is.

Don't worry about repairs or cleaning up the property. We'll buy your house in as-is condition — no matter how ugly or pretty it is, no matter the location, no matter the situation.

Common questions

What does "as-is" mean?
We buy your home in as-is condition — meaning you don't have to worry about doing repairs, fixing your home up, or getting it ready for showings. We'll handle all repairs, inspections, and cleanup after we purchase. We factor that into our offer of course, but it saves you the money and headache of getting your home ready to sell.
What does "all-cash offer" mean?
All cash means exactly that — all cash. Because we're real estate investors buying your home directly, we don't rely on traditional bank financing like retail homebuyers do. There's no risk of financing falling through or closing being delayed. When we make you an offer, that's the full amount you'll receive.
How "fast" is a "fast closing?"
After you send us information about your home, we can make you a no-obligation, fair cash offer in as little as 24 hours. After that, we can close at a local reputable title company in as little as 10 days. Compare that to the 30-90+ days it takes to close when listing traditionally.
What if I don't need a fast closing?
We work on your time frame. If you don't need a fast close — maybe you're making arrangements, sorting out finances, or exploring your future options — we can schedule the closing on the day that works best for you. 30, 60, 90 days from now — whatever you need.
Will I get a low-ball offer?
Our goal is to present you with the most fair offer possible. Unlike other buyers or the big tech giants, we're transparent about how we arrive at our offer amount. Our offer is based on what the property may be worth after the necessary improvements and upgrades. We'll walk you through the math line-by-line — no black box.
Is this even legit?
Yes. The real estate industry is rapidly changing, and thousands of Americans are exploring faster, easier, more transparent ways to sell. We're an alternative for those who don't have the time or ability to list their house on the market for top dollar. We're a BBB A+ Accredited Business, we've bought hundreds of homes, and we close at a local licensed title company — not a handshake-and-cash operation. We'll work with you to assess your situation, make a transparent all-cash offer, and close on your timeline.
