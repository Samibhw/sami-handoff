Why work with us

A small team doing real work

We're not a call-center sweatshop and we're not a Silicon Valley cosplay. We're a real business that helps real sellers in real situations — and we hire people who want to be part of that.

Remote-first, outcome-focused

Most roles are fully remote (US or Philippines-based). We care about results, not hours worked. If you can close deals, qualify leads, or process transactions from your kitchen table, we'd rather you do that than commute.

Ethics first — really

We don't do high-pressure sales, we don't mislead sellers, and we don't cut corners with legal docs. Our BBB A+ rating is something we've earned over 5+ years of doing business the right way — and we hire people who want to keep that reputation.

Growth path & real tenure

We promote internally. Our Dispositions Manager started as a cold-caller. Our Acquisitions Specialists have clear paths to Acquisitions Manager. We invest in training (scripts, systems, REI education) and expect you to grow with the business.

🇺🇸 US-Based Full-Time

Acquisitions Manager / Sales

Talk directly with sellers about their home and situation. Make offers. Negotiate creative structures. Close deals. This is our highest-impact role — if you're a strong communicator who loves connecting with people in real situations, this is where you'll thrive.

What you'll do
  • Respond to inbound seller leads within minutes, not hours
  • Conduct phone and video consultations with distressed sellers
  • Evaluate properties, run comps, structure offers
  • Negotiate cash, seller-financing, and subject-to deals
  • Hand off to Dispositions once under contract
What we're looking for
  • 3+ years in sales, real estate, or high-touch consulting
  • Comfortable with creative finance (or eager to learn)
  • English fluency (voice quality matters for distressed seller calls)
  • Comfortable on Zoom and phone all day
  • US-based (cannot be PH for this role)

🇺🇸 US-Based Full-Time

Dispositions Manager

Once we have a property under contract, it's your job to move it to a cash buyer (or onto our own portfolio). You manage our investor list, negotiate assignment fees, and coordinate the handoff to our title company.

What you'll do
  • Market new deals to our investor buyer list via SMS, email, and calls
  • Field buyer calls, negotiate assignment fees
  • Coordinate showings, inspections, and closings with title
  • Grow our buyer list through networking and systematic outreach
  • Report disposition metrics weekly
What we're looking for
  • 2+ years in real estate dispositions, wholesaling, or related role
  • Strong negotiator — comfortable pushing for higher AF
  • Familiarity with CRMs (we use GoHighLevel)
  • Fluent English — direct communication with investors
  • US-based preferred (PH candidates considered for night-shift market coverage)

Cold Calling & SMS Vetting Specialist

Our acquisitions engine starts with outreach. You make cold calls and send SMS campaigns to motivated seller lists, qualify the lead, and pass warm prospects to the Acquisitions team. This is a volume role with clear metrics.

What you'll do
  • 80-120 outbound calls/day to targeted seller lists
  • Run bulk SMS campaigns using our systems
  • Qualify motivation, timeline, and situation using our script
  • Log every touch in the CRM (detailed notes required)
  • Hand warm leads to Acquisitions within 1 hour of qualification
What we're looking for
  • Minimum 1 year cold-calling experience (real estate preferred)
  • Excellent English (conversational comfort with US sellers)
  • Thick skin — you'll hear "no" 90% of the time
  • Reliable high-speed internet + quiet workspace
  • Both US and Philippines candidates welcome

Transaction Coordinator

You're the glue between Acquisitions, Dispositions, and closing. Process contracts, coordinate with Closeline Settlements, manage the deal pipeline, and ensure nothing falls through the cracks between "under contract" and "check in hand."

What you'll do
  • Process purchase contracts and send for signature
  • Coordinate title work with Closeline Settlements
  • Track contingencies, deadlines, and closing dates
  • Communicate with sellers, buyers, title, and attorneys
  • Maintain clean deal pipeline in the CRM
What we're looking for
  • 2+ years as a transaction coordinator (REI or retail)
  • Familiarity with title processes, purchase contracts
  • Strong English writing (all email communication)
  • Extremely organized — this is a details role
  • Both US and Philippines candidates welcome
