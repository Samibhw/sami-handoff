⚠ Atlanta market reality check

Atlanta's market has slowed dramatically.

Drive through DeKalb, Fulton, Cobb, Gwinnett, or Henry County right now and you'll see it — "For Sale" signs up for 6+ months. Price reductions. Properties sitting for a year or more. Buyers are extremely picky, especially about condition. Homes in as-is condition struggle to sell at all. Sellers are either accepting prices they hate or watching listings rot.

This is where the structure of your sale matters more than ever. A traditional listing means 90–180+ days of waiting, 6% commissions, inspection repair demands, and typically a 5–15% price reduction from your asking.

For Atlanta sellers in today's market, creative finance structures often net more than cash or a traditional listing. Seller financing can give you higher total dollars plus monthly income. Novation captures retail upside without the listing pain. Subject-to solves equity problems cash can't touch. We'll walk you through all five structures on one call.
