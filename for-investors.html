Who this is for

Best for: proof of funds, closed 3+ deals in last 24 mo, ready to close in 10 days

Investor FAQ

Quick answers before you sign up

Is there a fee to join the investor list?

No. Joining is completely free. We make money on the spread between our acquisition price and the assignment fee — not from our buyer list.

How often will I get deals?

Depends on your market and deal-size filter. Active markets like Atlanta send 4–10 deals/month. Smaller markets might be 1–3/month. You control the volume via the filters you set at sign-up.

What does the "first verified offer gets the deal" mean?

When we send a deal to the list, it goes to everyone at once. The first investor to respond with a verified offer (proof of funds, signed contract, earnest money) gets the deal. We don't play games with multiple offers.

What if I'm a newer investor — will I still get access?

Yes. We welcome new investors as long as you're serious. We'll ask about your buying criteria and proof of funds or lending approval on your first deal. If you're legit, you're on the list.

Can I bring you deals (as a wholesaler)?

Absolutely. We JV with wholesalers we trust. If you have a deal in Atlanta, Tulsa, Tampa Bay, or Seattle you want to move, contact Sachan (our Dispositions Manager) directly at (866) 883-3769 or submit the signup form and note "JV deal bringing" in the comments.

How do I unsubscribe?

Reply STOP to any text message, or click unsubscribe on any email. You're out within 24 hours, no questions asked.
