⚠ Hernando & Pasco market reality

Florida's slowdown hit the Tampa Bay area hard.

Spring Hill, Brooksville, New Port Richey, Wesley Chapel — across Hernando and Pasco counties, homes are sitting. Insurance renewals doubled. Hurricane damage from Helene and Milton left many properties difficult to sell. Buyers are extremely picky — if your home shows any sign of wear, they demand steep concessions or walk away entirely. Traditional listings routinely run 6–9 months with significant price reductions.

This matters especially for retirees and inheritance situations. Waiting 9 months for a traditional sale means 9 more months of insurance premiums, another potential hurricane season, and continued uncertainty. For estates, it means the probate stays open longer than it should.

Creative structures work well in Florida right now. Seller financing gives retirees monthly income without waiting through another slow listing cycle. Cash offers close in 10 days — real speed, not "fast" in quotes. Subject-to helps sellers underwater from peak-2022 purchases. We have five paths. Veterans get an extra $250 at closing.

Seniors, retirees & families

Florida's where retirement homes get sold.

A huge percentage of our Florida work is with retirees downsizing, families handling a parent's estate, or veterans transitioning to assisted living. We slow down, welcome family and attorneys to the conversation, and work at your pace.

Jas, our dedicated Senior Family Advocate, is your point of contact for any situation involving a senior seller. She'll walk you and your family through every option — including seller financing, which often makes more sense for retirees than a lump-sum cash offer.
